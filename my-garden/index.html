绣球花 · 无尽夏Hydrangea · Endless Summer
Hydrangea macrophylla
花球直径12-18cm，一株同时开出粉紫色和蓝紫色两种颜色！这是因为土壤pH值刚好在中性附近，铝离子吸收不完全，蓝色和粉色同时表达。花量极大，约15-18个花球同时绽放，非常壮观。属于重复开花型，新老枝都能开花。 Flower heads 12-18cm across, blooming in both pink-purple and blue-purple on the same plant! Soil pH near neutral causes both colors to appear simultaneously. About 15-18 flower heads in bloom. Rebloomer — flowers on both old and new wood.
多年生Perennial 半阴Part Shade 爱喝水Loves Water 酸性土=蓝花Acid=Blue
🧡 养护🧡 Care： 保持土壤湿润（不能干透）；想保持蓝紫色用硫酸铝调酸；花谢后剪掉残花促进再开花；寿命20-50年。 Keep soil moist; use aluminum sulfate for blue color; deadhead spent blooms; lives 20-50 years.

杜鹃花 · 久留米品种Azalea · Kurume Variety
Rhododendron (Azalea)
鲜艳的玫红色，花量极丰，几乎覆盖整株。半重瓣品种，花瓣质感如绸缎。花朵直径约3-4cm，簇状密集排列形成花球效果。刚种下就已经盛花满枝，非常喜庆。 Vibrant magenta-red with profuse blooms nearly covering the entire plant. Semi-double petals with satin texture. Individual flowers 3-4cm, clustered in ball-like groups. Already in full bloom right after planting.
常绿Evergreen 多年生Perennial 耐阴Shade Tolerant 不需打药No Spray Needed
🧡 养护🧡 Care： 基本不用管！保持排水良好（别积水烂根）；偶尔施酸性肥；叶子发黄说明缺铁。波特兰气候完美适合杜鹃。 Very low maintenance! Good drainage is key; occasional acid fertilizer; yellow leaves = iron deficiency. Portland climate is perfect for azaleas.

地毯月季 · Drift RoseDrift Rose
Rosa 'Drift' series
粉红色小型月季，花朵密集簇生，花量惊人。Drift Rose系列是月季中的"战斗机"——抗病性强、持续开花、低矮匍匐生长，非常适合地被和花坛边缘。整个花季从春天到秋天不间断开花。 Pink miniature roses blooming in dense clusters. Drift Rose series are disease-resistant, continuous bloomers with low spreading habit. Perfect for borders and ground cover. Blooms from spring through fall non-stop.
持续开花Continuous Bloom 多年生Perennial 需打药Needs Spray 全日照Full Sun
🧡 养护🧡 Care： 唯一需要打药的花！定期喷杀虫药防蚜虫和黑斑病；花谢后剪掉促进再开花；全日照位置最好。 The only plant that needs spraying! Regular fungicide/insecticide for aphids and black spot; deadhead to rebloom; full sun preferred.

六倍利Lobelia
Lobelia erinus
蓝紫色小花密集簇生，色彩饱和度极高，像一块蓝色地毯。花型小巧精致，五瓣小花呈不规则唇形。种在花坛边缘做点缀，跟其他花形成漂亮的色彩对比。 Blue-purple tiny flowers in dense clusters, like a blue carpet. Delicate five-petaled irregular flowers. Planted at border edges for beautiful color contrast with other flowers.
一年生Annual 超好养Easy Care 不需打药No Spray 半阴Part Shade
🧡 养护🧡 Care： 种下就忘型！保持土壤湿润，花谢剪掉会再开一波。冬天可能冻死，明年花$5再买一盆。 Plant and forget! Keep moist, deadhead for rebloom. May not survive winter — just buy a new $5 pot next year.

紫玉兰Purple Magnolia
Magnolia liliiflora
春天最早开花的树之一，紫粉色大朵花苞挂在光秃枝条上，非常醒目。花瓣外侧紫红色，内侧淡粉白色。花落后才长叶子。地面上散落的花瓣也很有意境。 One of the first trees to bloom in spring, with purple-pink large flower buds on bare branches. Petals purple-red outside, pale pink-white inside. Leaves appear after flowers drop. Fallen petals create a poetic carpet.
春天开花Spring Bloom 乔木Tree 不需打理Low Maintenance

日本红枫 · 垂枝型Japanese Maple · Weeping
Acer palmatum var. dissectum
整棵树呈深酒红色，羽状细裂叶如瀑布般垂挂，非常优雅。是庭院中的视觉焦点，跟周围的绿色和白色形成强烈对比。秋天颜色会更红。 Deep wine-red foliage with finely dissected leaves cascading like a waterfall. A visual focal point contrasting beautifully with surrounding green and white. Turns even more red in fall.
