🔑 核心技术判断：TSV/HBM 决定合作边界 Key Technical Insight: TSV/HBM Defines Partnership Boundaries

为什么数据中心GPU几乎不可能成为代工合作入口？ Why Data Center GPUs Are Nearly Impossible to Enter as a Foundry Partner?

HBM的物理结构决定了一切：HBM（High Bandwidth Memory）本质上是多层DRAM Die通过TSV（硅通孔）垂直叠层的3D结构。GPU Die和HBM Stack必须封装在同一个interposer上，这就是CoWoS（Chip on Wafer on Substrate）。

HBM's physical architecture determines everything: HBM (High Bandwidth Memory) is fundamentally a 3D stack of DRAM dies connected through TSV (Through-Silicon Vias). The GPU die and HBM stack must be co-packaged on the same interposer — this is CoWoS (Chip on Wafer on Substrate).

CoWoS是TSMC的专有封装技术，TSMC同时控制：① 前道晶圆制造 ② CoWoS先进封装 ③ HBM集成。这三步无法拆分——TSMC不会把封装业务单独剥离给外部做。

CoWoS is TSMC's proprietary packaging technology. TSMC controls simultaneously: ① Front-end wafer fab ② CoWoS advanced packaging ③ HBM integration. These three steps cannot be separated — TSMC will not outsource packaging to third parties.

数据中心GPU因HBM/TSV/CoWoS被TSMC垂直整合，外部代工几乎无插入点。消费级GPU标准BGA封装、GDDR7独立上板，前道晶圆可独立合作，技术逻辑完全闭环。DC GPUs are vertically integrated by TSMC via HBM/TSV/CoWoS, leaving no external entry. Consumer GPUs use standard BGA, GDDR7 separate mount — front-end wafer is separable and cooperable. Technical logic is fully self-consistent.
2
消费级年出货44M台，月均晶圆需求~35k WPM，是数据中心的10倍Consumer GPU: 44M units/yr, ~35k WPM monthly — 10x DC volume
RTX 60全线N3P月均30,000-35,000 WPM，远超B200逻辑片的3,400 WPM。体量巨大且工艺链更短。RTX 60 full lineup N3P: 30,000-35,000 WPM monthly, far exceeding B200 logic wafers at 3,400 WPM. Massive volume with shorter supply chain.
3
Feynman确认A16（非A14），但仍需CoWoS+TSV，独立前道合作意义有限Feynman confirmed A16 (not A14), but still needs CoWoS+TSV — standalone front-end cooperation has limited value
TSMC A16含SPR背面供电，产能可能不足，NVIDIA探索Intel Foundry 18A。但Feynman仍是HBM+CoWoS体系，若无法提供完整封装方案，单独前道作用有限。TSMC A16 has SPR backside power, capacity may be insufficient, NVIDIA exploring Intel Foundry 18A. But Feynman still requires HBM+CoWoS — standalone front-end has limited value without full packaging capability.
4
IDDQ漏电控制是GPU代工首要技术指标，Flicker Noise次之IDDQ leakage control is the #1 technical metric; Flicker Noise is secondary
~800mm² 旗舰Die漏电极敏感，IDDQ BIN直接决定良率和收益。Flicker Noise主要影响PLL/SerDes，属中高要求。N3P大Die良率管理是竞争核心能力。~800mm² flagship die is extremely IDDQ-sensitive. IDDQ BIN directly determines yield and revenue. Flicker Noise affects PLL/SerDes, moderate-high requirement. N3P large die yield management is the core competitive capability.
5
双轨并行：消费级N3P主攻 + A16远期布局Dual-track: Consumer N3P primary + A16 long-term
近期主攻RTX 60 N3P（2026年工艺认证→2027年risk production→2027H2-2028量产），同步开展A16/A14 PDK预研作为远期布局。AMD和云厂商自研芯片作为补充备选。Near-term: RTX 60 N3P (2026 process qual → 2027 risk production → 2027H2-2028 ramp). Parallel: A16/A14 PDK R&D for long-term positioning. AMD and cloud custom chips as secondary alternatives.
