1. Nvidia锁定台积电先进封装产能，AI芯片下一个瓶颈已现
1. Nvidia Locks Up TSMC Advanced Packaging Capacity
Nvidia已包下台积电大部分CoWoS（Chip-on-Wafer-on-Substrate）先进封装产能，用于其新一代Blackwell和Vera Rubin AI芯片的大规模生产。这一举动导致AMD、Intel等其他客户面临封装排期严重紧张的困境。分析师指出，先进封装已成为AI芯片供应链中比晶圆制造更紧迫的瓶颈，台积电正加速扩产但短期内仍供不应求。来源：Reuters / Bloomberg
Nvidia has secured the majority of TSMC's CoWoS (Chip-on-Wafer-on-Substrate) advanced packaging capacity for its next-gen Blackwell and Vera Rubin AI chips. This leaves AMD, Intel, and other customers facing severe packaging scheduling constraints. Analysts note that advanced packaging has become a more pressing bottleneck than wafer fabrication itself. TSMC is accelerating expansion but supply remains short of demand in the near term. Source: Reuters / Bloomberg
2. Intel加入马斯克TerraFab AI芯片项目
2. Intel Joins Musk's TerraFab AI Chip Project
Intel宣布加入Elon Musk主导的TerraFab AI芯片制造联盟，与SpaceX、Tesla、xAI等公司深度合作，计划在德克萨斯州Austin建设大规模AI芯片制造工厂。该项目旨在打破对台积电的依赖，实现AI芯片的本土化生产。Intel将贡献其18A先进制程技术和封装能力，预计2027年首批芯片下线。来源：WSJ / The Verge
Intel has joined Elon Musk's TerraFab AI chip manufacturing alliance, collaborating with SpaceX, Tesla, and xAI to build a large-scale AI chip fab in Austin, Texas. The project aims to break dependence on TSMC and achieve domestic AI chip production. Intel will contribute its 18A process technology and packaging capabilities, with first chips expected in 2027. Source: WSJ / The Verge
3. AMD推理市场加速渗透，数据中心业务同比增39%
3. AMD Accelerates Inference Market Penetration
AMD最新财报显示数据中心业务Q4同比增长39%，GPU在AI推理场景中的采用率持续上升。随着企业从模型训练转向推理部署，AMD Instinct系列GPU凭借性价比优势获得了更多云服务商和企业客户的青睐。公司还推出了新的MI350系列推理优化芯片，直接挑战Nvidia在推理市场的地位。来源：CNBC / Seeking Alpha
AMD's latest earnings show data center revenue up 39% YoY in Q4, with GPU adoption in AI inference scenarios rising steadily. As enterprises shift from training to inference deployment, AMD Instinct GPUs are winning more cloud and enterprise clients on price-performance. The new MI350 inference-optimized chips directly challenge Nvidia's inference dominance. Source: CNBC / Seeking Alpha
4. Nvidia自由现金流达$965亿，计划$100亿收购扩张AI版图
4. Nvidia Free Cash Flow Hits $96.5B, Plans $10B Acquisition Spree
Nvidia 2026财年自由现金流达到惊人的965亿美元，公司正计划投入超过100亿美元进行战略性收购，从芯片设计向全栈AI基础设施延伸。潜在收购目标涵盖AI软件平台、数据中心冷却技术、以及AI网络解决方案领域。华尔街分析认为，Nvidia正试图构建一个覆盖硬件、软件和服务的完整AI生态系统。来源：Financial Times / Bloomberg
Nvidia's FY2026 free cash flow reached a staggering $96.5B, and the company plans to deploy over $10B in strategic acquisitions, expanding from chip design into full-stack AI infrastructure. Potential targets include AI software platforms, data center cooling tech, and AI networking solutions. Wall Street sees Nvidia building a complete AI ecosystem spanning hardware, software, and services. Source: Financial Times / Bloomberg
5. 先进封装成为AI芯片供应链新瓶颈
5. Advanced Packaging Becomes the New AI Chip Supply Chain Bottleneck
随着AI芯片对高带宽内存（HBM）和CoWoS封装的需求激增，先进封装已成为制约整个行业产能的关键瓶颈。台积电正在加速扩产CoWoS产能，目标在2026年底前将月产能提升至8万片以上，但仍然无法满足所有客户的需求。三星和Intel也在加紧布局先进封装能力，但技术成熟度和产能规模与台积电仍有差距。来源：Nikkei Asia / Reuters
As AI chip demand for HBM and CoWoS packaging surges, advanced packaging has become the key bottleneck constraining industry capacity. TSMC is accelerating CoWoS expansion, targeting 80K+ wafers/month by end of 2026, but still can't satisfy all customers. Samsung and Intel are also building advanced packaging capabilities, but lag behind TSMC in technology maturity and scale. Source: Nikkei Asia / Reuters
