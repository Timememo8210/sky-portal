1. 中国AI芯片特殊供应渠道终止，TSMC加速美国扩张China's Special AI Chip Supply Ends; TSMC Accelerates US Expansion

DIGITIMES报道，中国此前通过特殊渠道获取的AI芯片供应已正式终止。与此同时，TSMC宣布计划在亚利桑那州建设12座晶圆厂和4座先进封装设施，总投资额达$1650亿，成为美国历史上最大的半导体投资计划。台湾方面已同意向美国投资$500亿作为配套。DIGITIMES reports China's special AI chip supply channels have officially ended. Meanwhile, TSMC plans 12 fabs and 4 advanced packaging facilities in Arizona, totaling $165B investment — the largest semiconductor investment in US history. Taiwan has agreed to a $500B配套 investment.

来源：Source: DIGITIMES / Tom's Hardware

Growth driven not only by AI chips but also memory chips (revenue tripling). BofA names Nvidia, Broadcom, and AMD as key beneficiaries, raising forecast by $300B in four months.

Fabricated using standard commercial methods, it reportedly solves a century-old precision problem in analog computing. If verified, this could reshape the AI chip landscape, though industry experts remain skeptical about reproducibility.

来源：Source: Gartner
