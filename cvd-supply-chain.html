溅射靶材 (Sputtering Targets)Sputtering Targets

靶材主要用于PVD工艺，但部分CVD工艺（如MOCVD的金属有机源、CVD用钨WF6等）也需高纯金属材料。国产化率持续提升。Targets mainly for PVD, but some CVD processes (MOCVD metal-organic sources, W-CVD WF6) also need high-purity metals. Localization rate steadily improving.

特种气体 (Specialty Gases)Specialty Gases

CVD核心耗材——直接影响薄膜质量和particle水平CVD Core Consumable — Directly impacts film quality and particle levels

CVD关键特气：SiH4(硅烷)、TEOS、NH₃、N₂O、NF₃(清洗)、WF₆(钨CVD)、TDMAT/TDEAT(TiN CVD)等。国产化率约30-40%。Key CVD gases: SiH4, TEOS, NH₃, N₂O, NF₃(cleaning), WF₆(W-CVD), TDMAT/TDEAT(TiN CVD). Localization ~30-40%.

石英制品 (Quartz Components)Quartz Components

CVD反应腔核心耗材——石英反应管、石英舟等。直接影响particle控制。寿命有限，需定期更换。Core CVD chamber consumable — quartz reaction tubes, boats. Directly affects particle control. Limited lifetime, periodic replacement needed.
