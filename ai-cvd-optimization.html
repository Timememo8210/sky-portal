  • Fabtex™ Yield Optimizer (SEMICON West 2025.10发布)
  • CVP David Fried领衔：传统方法"慢、迭代、昂贵"CVP David Fried: Traditional methods "slow, iterative, expensive"

  • 案例：某头部器件制造商NPI阶段，虚拟硅方法提前发现系统缺陷，避免了所有wafer到end-of-line才被e-test发现，节省数月时间和大量scrap成本Case: A leading device manufacturer at NPI, virtual silicon caught systematic defects early, avoiding all wafers reaching end-of-line e-test, saving months and significant scrap costs
  • 核心突破：业界首个用nominal虚拟工艺模型+fab数据+AI/ML推荐量测target变更的工具。可同时解决多个yield detractor（非传统Pareto逐一击破）Breakthrough: Industry's first tool using nominal virtual process model + fab data + AI/ML to recommend metrology target changes. Addresses multiple yield detractors simultaneously (not traditional sequential Pareto)

  • 效果：fab整体效率提升30%（2026.02报告），相当于每座fab每年节省数百万美元Impact: Overall fab efficiency +30% (Feb 2026 report), equivalent to saving millions per fab per year
