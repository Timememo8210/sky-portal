🤔 额外考量Additional Considerations

选择Turo的理由Why Turo Wins

  • ✅ 轻度用户最划算 — 一年只用3-4个月，养车不如租车✅ Best for light users — only need a car 3-4 months/year
  • ✅ 永远开最新款 — 2-3年后新的FSD出来，租新车就行✅ Always latest model — rent new cars when FSD upgrades arrive
  • ✅ Model X 日常够用 — 你一个人在波特兰不需要第二辆车✅ Model X sufficient daily — don't need a second car solo in Portland
  • ✅ 资金灵活 — $7,000投资 > 停在车库里贬值✅ Capital flexibility — $7,000 invested > depreciating in garage
  • ✅ 无固定支出 — 不用月供、不买保险、不付注册费✅ No fixed costs — no payments, insurance, or registration

需要注意的风险Risks to Consider

  • ⚠️ Turo 车源可能临时被订走 — 提前预订⚠️ Turo cars may be booked — reserve early
  • ⚠️ 唐艺来的时候用车需求重叠 — Model X + Turo 租车可解决⚠️ Overlapping car needs when wife visits — Model X + Turo solves this
  • ⚠️ 基金收益不保证 — 20-30%是乐观估计，可能5-10%⚠️ Fund returns not guaranteed — 20-30% is optimistic, could be 5-10%
  • ⚠️ Odyssey 卖了就回不来了 — 但10年旧车，不卖也会继续贬值⚠️ Selling Odyssey is irreversible — but 10yr car keeps depreciating anyway
