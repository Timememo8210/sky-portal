2025.10 — 持续至今ongoing Foundry

18A正式进入量产阶段，Fab 52完成首批晶圆制造18A Enters High-Volume Manufacturing; Fab 52 Completes First Wafer Lot

Intel 18A于2025年10月进入高量产(HVM)，亚利桑那州Fab 52已完成首批晶圆"run the lot"。该节点集成RibbonFET全环栅晶体管与业界首创PowerVia背面供电技术，较Intel 3提升约15%性能/瓦、30%芯片密度。目前良率每月约提升7%，目标年底前达到商业化可行水平。Intel 18A entered HVM in October 2025. Fab 52 in Arizona has completed its first "run the lot." The node integrates RibbonFET GAA transistors and industry-first PowerVia backside power delivery, offering ~15% perf/watt and 30% density improvement over Intel 3. Yields are improving ~7% monthly, targeting commercially viable levels by year-end.
