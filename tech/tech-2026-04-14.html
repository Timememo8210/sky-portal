硅光子（Silicon Photonics）是什么？为什么AI芯片需要它？What is Silicon Photonics? Why do AI chips need it?

想象一下，城市里所有车都挤在高速公路上——这就是现在AI芯片之间的数据传输瓶颈：铜线的带宽已经到顶了。硅光子技术用"光"代替"电"来传输数据，就像把高速公路升级成光速铁路。TSMC的COUPE平台和三星的CPO方案都在争夺这个赛道，目标是用光纤连接GPU和内存，带宽提升数十倍，功耗大幅下降。2026-2029年将是硅光子从实验室走向量产的关键窗口。Think of cars jammed on a highway—that's today's AI chip interconnect bottleneck: copper wires have hit their bandwidth ceiling. Silicon photonics replaces electricity with light for data transfer, like upgrading a highway to a lightspeed railway. TSMC's COUPE platform and Samsung's CPO solution are both racing to connect GPUs and memory with fiber optics, boosting bandwidth by orders of magnitude while slashing power. 2026-2029 will be the critical window for silicon photonics to move from lab to mass production.
